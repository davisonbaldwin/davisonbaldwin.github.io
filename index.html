Davis Baldwin is a product designer and builder who completed a Bachelor of Humanities and Arts at Carnegie Mellon University (May 2026), combining Music Technology and Hispanic Studies. He has worked as a software engineering intern at Gamma Technologies and as a research fellow at Carnegie Mellon studying improvisational composition. His portfolio centers on an interactive planetarium built from real astronomical catalogs, alongside a live stock-analysis web app and a body of original music technology.
