WORLD 03 · MUSIC TECHNOLOGY · SIX WORKS

Music technology

Six original works composed, performed, and produced with technology at the center: a choral duology, an orchestral ballad, a dance film, a string trio, and two animated collaborations. Each chapter keeps its own palette, type, and design, built from the work itself.

CHAPTER 1 A Soul’s Journey CHORAL DUOLOGY One page that travels from rest into the sun: “Rest my soul” in the light, “Dame sol” in Franco’s dark. A one-man choir’s passage, improvised entirely by ear. CHAPTER 2 Sometimes in the world BALLAD · POP + ORCHESTRA A pop-meets-orchestra ballad on the depths of loss and tragedy. The full orchestra built in software, every instrument digital, one voice at the center of it. CHAPTER 3 Time to Fly SONG + DANCE FILM The breakthrough of a man who realizes he can no longer let fear dictate his life. Choreographed, filmed, and directed with eight collaborators. CHAPTER 4 You won’t love me back SONG + FILM · STRING TRIO A married man realizes his wife no longer loves him, and comes to accept that more than anything he wants her to be happy. CHAPTER 5 Life’s Meaning SONG + ANIMATION An investigation into the purpose of our existence: voice, piano, and synth inside Caleb Worcester’s animated world, a piano at the edge of a pond at dusk. CHAPTER 6 Supernova Cloud INSTRUMENTAL An otherworldly composition exploring the universe of polytonality, improvised in the very moment it was played, with the film animated by Caleb Worcester.
